7. PHASE 4 FEATURE ENGINEERING PLAN — BUREAU TABLE
----------------------------------------------------------------
All features aggregated to SK_ID_CURR grain

A. Coverage & volume features
   FLAG_NO_BUREAU_HISTORY      — 1 if no bureau records (thin-file)
   COUNT_BUREAU_TOTAL          — total number of bureau credits
   COUNT_BUREAU_ACTIVE         — number of active credits
   COUNT_BUREAU_CLOSED         — number of closed credits
   COUNT_BUREAU_BAD_DEBT       — number of bad debt credits
   COUNT_BUREAU_SOLD           — number of sold credits
   RATIO_ACTIVE_TO_TOTAL       — active / total credits

B. Risk flags (mandatory)
   FLAG_HAS_BAD_DEBT_BUREAU    — any bad debt credit ever
   FLAG_EVER_OVERDUE           — any credit day overdue > 0
   FLAG_HAS_MICROLOAN          — any microloan in bureau
   FLAG_HAS_MORTGAGE           — any mortgage in bureau
   FLAG_HAS_CREDIT_CARD        — any credit card in bureau

C. Overdue & delinquency features
   MAX_CREDIT_DAY_OVERDUE      — max days overdue across all credits
   SUM_CREDIT_DAY_OVERDUE      — total days overdue
   MAX_AMT_CREDIT_MAX_OVERDUE  — max overdue amount ever
   SUM_AMT_CREDIT_SUM_OVERDUE  — total current overdue amount

D. Credit exposure features
   SUM_AMT_CREDIT_SUM          — total bureau credit exposure
   SUM_AMT_CREDIT_SUM_DEBT     — total outstanding debt
   MEAN_AMT_CREDIT_SUM_DEBT    — average outstanding debt per credit
   DEBT_TO_CREDIT_RATIO        — total debt / total credit sum

9. KEY INSIGHTS FOR MODEL DEVELOPMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. CREDIT_DAY_OVERDUE is the strongest signal in bureau table —
   18.21% bad rate when overdue vs 7.79% when not (+10.42pp).
   FLAG_EVER_OVERDUE is a mandatory Phase 4 feature.

2. CREDIT_ACTIVE = Bad debt has 20% bad rate — 2.5x population.
   FLAG_HAS_BAD_DEBT_BUREAU is a mandatory Phase 4 feature.

3. Microloan credit type has 20.64% bad rate — signals financial
   stress and limited access to mainstream credit.

4. Mortgage has 5% bad rate — strongest protective signal in bureau.
   Property ownership and long-term credit stability.

5. Thin-file applicants (14.3%) have 10.12% bad rate vs 7.73%
   for applicants with bureau history (+2.39pp).
   FLAG_NO_BUREAU_HISTORY is a mandatory Phase 4 feature.

6. CREDIT_CURRENCY is near-zero variance (99.9% currency 1) —
   expected to screen out on IV in Phase 5.

7. AMT_CREDIT_MAX_OVERDUE missing (64.73%) is INFORMATIVE —
   missing means never overdue. Do NOT impute with mean/median.
